Our Team

We are a group of developers that found ourselves struggeling to find motivation to perform some sort of exercise daily.
So we decided that we were going to solve this problem by creating a game to help people in the same shoes that we are in to get off the couch
and start heading outside to walk around and solve captivating mysteries. We also acknowledge the importance of the environment
so the designed our game for the outdoors, so that you can enjoy the game outside, while saving money and energy at home.
Our mission is simple: Solve Murders, Exercise, Save Energy
