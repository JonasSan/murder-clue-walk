Clue 1

You (the detective) show up at the crime scene. There is a dead woman lying on the ground. Next to her is a knife, but there doesn't seem to be any blood. There are no signs of a struggle either, but the woman does not appear to have died of natural causes. Investigating the surroundings you find some footprints. You decide to pick up the knife and follow the footprints.
