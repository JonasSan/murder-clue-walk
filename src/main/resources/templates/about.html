Our Team

We are a group of developers that found ourselves struggling to find motivation to perform some sort of daily exercise.
So we decided that we were going to solve this problem by creating a game to help people who were in the same shoes, by getting them of the couch
and start heading outside to walk around and solve captivating mysteries. We also acknowledge the importance of the environment
so we designed our game for usage outdoors, so that you can enjoy the game outside, while saving money and keeping the electric bill at home low.
Our mission is simple: Solve Murders, Exercise, Save Energy
